About Us

The Islamic Cultural Centre (ICC) is a student organization at the College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET) dedicated to fostering a sense of community, spirituality, and service. We provide essential services like the student mess and organize cultural and educational events to enrich the lives of students on campus.
